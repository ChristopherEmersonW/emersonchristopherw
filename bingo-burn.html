Bingo Burn Mobile App

A playful fitness app that motivates users to stay active through game-like bingo challenges.

Project Overview

01

Project Goals

The goal of Bingo Burn is to create an engaging and interactive fitness experience that encourages users to maintain an active lifestyle through fun challenges.

Design Process

The design process involved user research, wireframing, prototyping, and user testing to ensure the app meets the needs and preferences of its target audience.

Key Features

The app includes various bingo challenges, progress tracking, and social sharing features to enhance user engagement and motivation.
