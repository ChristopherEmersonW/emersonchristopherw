Who Am I

Hi, I’m Christopher Emerson, a senior at Michigan State University studying Experience Architecture with a focus on UX and product design. I’m passionate about creating experiences that connect people and technology in meaningful ways, whether through digital interfaces, wayfinding systems, or inclusive design strategies. I love working with others to solve problems, sketch ideas, build prototypes, and bring concepts to life. I’m comfortable using tools like Adobe Creative Suite and Figma. I’m always looking for ways to blend creative thinking with thoughtful strategy.

What excites me most about product design is its ever-evolving nature and the constant opportunity to learn and adapt. I am particularly drawn to the intersection of technology and human behavior, exploring how design can solve complex problems while maintaining empathy at its core. As I look forward to post-graduation opportunities, I am eager to apply my skills to impactful projects that challenge me to think critically, collaborate effectively, and push creative boundaries.

Outside of Work

When I’m not working or studying, I’m usually staying active, whether that’s playing soccer, hitting the gym, or just getting outside. I really value time with friends and family, and I make space for those connections whenever I can. Lately, I’ve also been diving into the world of generative AI—reading, experimenting, and learning how it’s shaping creativity, design, and everyday life. It’s a fun mix of movement, curiosity, and connection that keeps me balanced..
